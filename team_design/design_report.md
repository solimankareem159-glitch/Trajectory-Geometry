Based on the initial team design and the critique, the team structure has been refined to address potential bottlenecks and improve focus on reproducibility. The 'Data Alchemist' and 'Experiment Architect' roles will collaborate closely, with the Experiment Architect taking primary responsibility for data generation pipelines. The 'Manuscript Artisan' and 'Dissemination Strategist' roles have been merged into a single 'Communication & Dissemination Specialist' role to streamline the communication process. A 'Reproducibility Advocate' role has been added, integrated into the Statistical Oracle's responsibilities, to ensure the experiments are easily reproducible. The PI's responsibilities have been slightly distributed by empowering the Claim Defender to proactively identify and address potential criticisms, freeing up the PI to focus on high-level strategy and resource management.

**Revised Team:**

*   **Codebase Janitor:** (Same as before)
*   **Experiment Architect:** (Collaborates with Data Alchemist, responsible for data pipelines)
*   **Data Alchemist:** (Collaborates with Experiment Architect, focuses on data quality and augmentation)
*   **Hidden Layer Cartographer:** (Same as before)
*   **Statistical Oracle/Reproducibility Advocate:** (Responsible for statistical analysis AND ensuring reproducibility)
*   **Communication & Dissemination Specialist:** (Merges Manuscript Artisan and Dissemination Strategist)
*   **Claim Defender:** (Proactively identifies and addresses criticisms)
*   **Principal Investigator (PI):** (Oversees project, manages resources, defends claims, delegates more proactively)
